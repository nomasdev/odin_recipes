Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika, divided
  • 3 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 green bell pepper, choped
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon minces garlic
  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cip white wine
  • 1/8 teaspoon saffron
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parseley

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Season chicken with a 1/4 teaspoon of salt, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, and 1/4 teaspoon paprika

  2. Step 2

    Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add seasoned chick; cook and stir until no longer pink in the center and golden brown on all sides, about 10 minutes. Transfer chicken onto a plate; set aside.

  3. Step 3

    Add green pepper, onions, and garlic to the same skillet; cook and stir for 5 minues. Ad rice; cook and stir until rice is opaque, 1 to 2 minutes, Stir in broth, tomatoes, white wine, and saffron. Stir in remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teasoon pepper, and 1/4 teaspoon paprike; bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 20 minutes

  4. Step

    Add chicken and stir until heated through. Stir in parsley and serve.
