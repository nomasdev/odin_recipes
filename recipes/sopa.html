Ingredients

  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 small onion, chopped/
  • 1 large clove garlic, coarsely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 (7 ounce) package fideo pasta, uncooked
  • 3 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • coarse salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Place tomatoes, onion, and garlic into a blender; plus several times to get the mixture, then blend until smooth, 30 seconds to 1 minute

  2. Step 2

    Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-low heat; stir in fideo pasta. Fry pasta gently, stiring often, until golden brown, 2 to 5 minutes. Remove from heat

  3. Step 3

    Pour tomato mixture into a large saucepan; stire in chicken broth and noodles. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until noodles are tender, 7 to 8 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Season with salt and pepper; stire in cilantro and cook until warmed through
